Fairways & Bluewater Golf and Country Club

Step into a place where man’s creative innovations meet nature’s artistry. We take pride in being the premier eco-friendly vacation destination in the Philippines. Wake up to the sight of the perfect sunrise casting golden shadows at the endless greens. Catch a breath of fresh air while riding in our environment-friendly electric jeeps. Sink your feet into powdery white sand while gazing at clear azure waters that seem to meet the blue sky. And since this is your piece of paradise, you can indulge in our world-class service and experience our people’s unrivaled hospitality. We take note of all your needs as we ensure that you are as pampered and comfortable as possible throughout your stay with us. At Fairways and Bluewater, we redefine lifestyle.
